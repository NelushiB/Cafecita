About us

Our Story

Cafecita was born in 2015 out of a deep passion for high-quality coffee and a desire to bring a slice of the world’s finest coffee culture to Milan. The journey began when our founder, Nelushi, embarked on a life-changing trip to Colombia. There, she was captivated by the rich, aromatic coffees and the vibrant culture surrounding coffee production.

Returning to Italy, Nelushi was determined to create a place where she could share this passion. She spent years perfecting her craft, learning from coffee farmers and baristas around the world. In 2015, her dream came to life with the opening of Cafecita.

Our Philosophy and Mission

At Cafecita, we believe that great coffee starts with the bean. We source only the finest, ethically-grown beans from sustainable farms across the globe.

We are committed to sustainability and ethical practices, ensuring that every cup of coffee you enjoy at Cafecita supports fair trade and environmentally friendly farming methods.

Our Atmosphere

Step into Cafecita and you'll find a warm, inviting atmosphere. Our space is designed to be a retreat from the hustle and bustle of Milan. Whether you’re looking for a quiet place to work, a cozy corner to read, or a lively spot to catch up with friends, Cafecita offers the perfect setting.
